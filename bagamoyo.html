Bagamoyo Coastal Tour

.Bagamoyo is a historic coastal town once used as a major East African trade port. It is known for its old colonial buildings, slave trade history, beautiful beaches, and rich Swahili culture

Historical Town Exploration

.Visitors explore ancient buildings, narrow streets, and historical landmarks that tell stories of trade, culture, and early missionaries

Slave Market & Museum Visit

A key highlight is visiting the Bagamoyo Slave Market and the Caravan Serai Museum..

Kaole Ruins (13th Century)

Located just outside the town, the Kaole Ruins feature ancient mosques, tombs, and coral stone structures from the 1200s. It is a peaceful archaeological site surrounded by nature and history..

Beautiful White-Sand Beaches

Bagamoyo offers quiet and relaxing beaches perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and evening walks. The calm coastal atmosphere makes it a great escape from busy city life..

Traditional Fishing Village Tour

Visitors can experience local coastal life by touring fishing villages. Here you can see fishermen preparing nets, wooden boats, and daily activities of the Swahili community..

Arts & Culture at Bagamoyo College of Arts

Bagamoyo is known for its vibrant art and music culture. The College of Arts offers traditional dance performances, sculptures, painting exhibitions, and cultural shows.

Sunset Ocean Views

The coastline offers breathtaking sunsets where the sky meets the Indian Ocean. This experience is perfect for relaxation and photography.
