Join Arusha Park

.Overview: This park is located in northern Tanzania, near the city of Arusha. It includes Mount Meru, lush forests, flowing rivers, and small hills.

Wildlife

Animals found here include giraffes, buffaloes, elephants, lions, various birds, and other wildlife.

Scenery

The landscape is a mix of forests, mountains, and small lakes, offering excellent opportunities for photography and nature walks..

Tourist Services

Short safaris, mountain trekking, picnics, and wildlife viewing opportunities..

Highlights

.Though relatively small, it offers stunning scenery and abundant wildlife, ideal for short group or family trips

Activities

Short safaris, trekking, picnics, and wildlife viewing..

Location

Located in northern Tanzania, about 35 km from Arusha city. It is easily accessible for day trips from the city or Kilimanjaro International Airport.

Size

Covers approximately 137 km² of diverse landscapes, including savannah, forests, rivers, and volcanic cones.

Mount Meru

The park surrounds Mount Meru (4,566m), Tanzania’s second-highest peak, which offers challenging hiking opportunities and stunning panoramic views..
